ABOUT US

Dawn D Eidelman

 

Dedicated to serving clients pursuing their best possible life, Dawn Eidelman is delighted to join the AWAI community as a copywriter, and info-marketer.

 

Clients benefit from her 30 years of experience as a humanities professor, curriculum designer, content marketer, social entrepreneur, speaker and business executive.

 

Dawn has developed info-marketing content, web pages, proposals, video scripts and a full-spectrum of copywriting materials for decades as an entrepreneur. She co-founded Mosaica Education in 1997 and sold it in 2015.

 

A multilingual specialist in the Humanities, Dawn developed a Pre-K through Grade 12 liberal arts program, and all training materials to scale the program in over 100 charter and online schools on three continents. The Paragon Curriculum she created for Mosaica Education has garnered numerous awards and propelled over 100,000 students to excellence around the globe.

 

Now with AWAI training, Dawn Eidelman serves as a copywriter, info-marketer, course designer, and consultant to promote the work of thought leaders dedicated to transforming the quality of their clients' lives – with vibrant health, financial freedom, thriving relationships and geographic freedom.
